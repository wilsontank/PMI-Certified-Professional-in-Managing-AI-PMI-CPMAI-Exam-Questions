<h2><strong>What Is the PMI Certified Professional in Managing AI (PMI-CPMAI)&trade;?</strong></h2>

<p>PMI has officially introduced the PMI Certified Professional in Managing AI (PMI-CPMAI) certification as the next-generation standard for AI project management. This new credential replaces the former Cognitive Project Management in AI (CPMAI v7) certification and elevates the framework into a globally recognized benchmark for successfully managing AI-based initiatives.</p>

<p>Built on the established CPMAI Methodology, PMI-CPMAI focuses not on general project management, but on the unique skill set required to deliver AI, machine learning, intelligent automation, and advanced analytics projects. It equips professionals with a vendor-neutral, repeatable process to manage AI initiatives from ideation through full operationalization.</p>

<h2><strong>Why PMI Modernized CPMAI: Understanding the Shift from CPMAI v7 to PMI-CPMAI</strong></h2>

<p>As organizations integrate AI across operations, PMI identified several new capabilities that AI project managers must possess&mdash;capabilities not fully addressed in older methodologies. The shift to PMI-CPMAI ensures the certification aligns with:</p>

<ul>
	<li>The complexity of AI data lifecycles, data governance, and model accuracy</li>
	<li>The need for ethical, trustworthy, and compliant AI development</li>
	<li>Cross-disciplinary collaboration across data science, engineering, and business teams</li>
	<li>Rapid AI evolution driven by GenAI, ML, and automation technologies</li>
	<li>Industry demand for professionals who can drive real-world AI outcomes</li>
</ul>

<p>By modernizing the credential, PMI ensures that PMI-CPMAI serves as the <strong>gold standard</strong> for AI project leadership worldwide.</p>

<h2><strong>What the PMI-CPMAI Exam Evaluates: Skills for Managing AI Projects End-to-End</strong></h2>

<p>The PMI-CPMAI exam is designed around real-world AI delivery challenges. Each question is built and reviewed by experienced AI specialists, ensuring candidates are assessed on their ability to apply the CPMAI methodology effectively. The exam measures proficiency in:</p>

<ul>
	<li>Translating business needs into AI-ready problem statements</li>
	<li>Assessing data sources, quality, and governance requirements</li>
	<li>Managing iterative AI model development with a focus on evaluation and accountability</li>
	<li>Operationalizing models and deploying them into production pipelines</li>
	<li>Ensuring all AI solutions align with responsible and ethical AI principles</li>
</ul>

<p>This vendor-neutral approach ensures PMI-CPMAI is applicable to AI projects across industries, tools, and platforms.</p>
